Show Errors

Causes subrecords containing errors to highlight. There are two types of errors:

  • the current record contains an improper field value
  • a record is missing a required subrecord.

Records that are missing required subrecords will show faint yellow items in dashed boxes. These are "ghost records" that tell you what kind of record you need. The application will not allow you to save files that contain records with errors in record field values. However, it will allow you to save partial files, even if the data model designers have decided that a record requires a certain subrecord.

Press Clear to clear the display.

Operations

Show errors

  1. Click on the "Options" menu.
  2. Click on the "Show Errors" option. The tree should highlight errors in the tree.
