Show Changes

Show changes will highlight records in the tree that have been added or modified since the last time you pressed Save or Save As

Try making a change of some kind and pressing this button. You should see changed items highlighted either by a blue folder or a blue bullet.

Press Clear to clear the display.

Operations

Show changes

  1. Click on the "Options" menu.
  2. Click on the "Show Changes" button. You should see the tree highlight modified or added items either with a blue dot or blue folder.
