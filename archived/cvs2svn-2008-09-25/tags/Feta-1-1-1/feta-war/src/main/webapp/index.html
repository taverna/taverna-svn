The myGrid Feta Semantic Service Discovery Engine

penpecip@cs.man.ac.uk, 19 January, 2006

This is the index page for an installation of the myGrid semantic service discovery engine Feta version1.0. This service is used to query over a group of service descriptions. The Feta engine can be queried by the help of Feta Client, which is a GUI plug-in to Taverna.

If there are problems with the service, you may want to check that the underlying Axis engine is correctly configured, checking here will give you some basic checks on library status although these are not sufficient of themselves to guarantee functionality.

Any questions about this service please email Pinar Alper (penpecip@cs.man.ac.uk)
