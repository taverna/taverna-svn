Clear

Removes all special highlighting and other visual decorations from the tree. The operation has the following effects:

  • removes red highlighting from error-prone items
  • removes yellow highlighted items that represent missing required subrecords
  • removes blue highlighting for changed items
  • removes purple highlighting for search results

Pressing "Clear" will not eliminate errors from a file, and will not eliminate the application's memory of changes being made. It simply resets the tree display.

Operations

Clear the tree display

  1. Click on the "Options" menu.
  2. Click the "Clear" button. All highlighting representing missing subrecords, records containing errors, changed records or records appearing in search results.
