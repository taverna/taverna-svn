About Pedro...

Pedro was originally developed to serve as an XML editing tool for the proteomics community associated with COGEME.

The work was funded by:

  • the COGEME project of the BBSRC's Investigating Gene Function Initiative
    • The North-West Regional e-Science Centre (ESNW) of the UK e-Science Programme
  • Development Group
  • Testing Group
  • Alpha Testers
  • Beta Testers
  • Scientific Advisors
